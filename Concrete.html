    Concrete & Epoxy Solutions for UAE Construction Industry

    Concrete Solutions for Construction Projects

    JESR Al SEYOUH supplies a comprehensive range of concrete solutions for construction projects across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and the wider UAE:

    • Concrete Repair Materials: For fixing cracks, spalling, and structural damage
    • Concrete Additives: Accelerators, retarders, plasticizers for specialized applications
    • Grouting Compounds: For anchor bolts, machinery bases, and structural repairs
    • Surface Hardeners: For increasing concrete durability and abrasion resistance
    • Waterproofing Compounds: For basement, terrace, and water tank applications

    Epoxy Flooring & Coating Systems

    Our epoxy solutions are ideal for various industrial and commercial applications in the UAE:

    • Epoxy Floor Coatings: For factories, warehouses, and commercial spaces
    • Self-Leveling Epoxy: For seamless, hygienic floor surfaces
    • Anti-Slip Epoxy: For safety in wet areas and industrial facilities
    • Chemical Resistant Epoxy: For laboratories and chemical plants
    • Decorative Epoxy: For showrooms, retail spaces, and architectural applications

    Applications in UAE Construction

    Our concrete and epoxy products are used in various UAE construction applications:

    • High-rise building construction and repair
    • Industrial flooring for factories and warehouses
    • Commercial flooring for malls and offices
    • Infrastructure projects (bridges, roads, tunnels)
    • Residential construction and renovation
